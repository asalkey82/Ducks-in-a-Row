# DUCKS IN A ROW
You are the DUCK MESSIAH! Collect your
followers and lead them to the sancturary with out 
losing them to the DUCK EATER!


# Movement:
WASD keys for up, down, left, and right. 
left shift - sprint,
f - drop ducklings
Touch the ducks in order to get them to follow you. 
Drop them off at the pond to keep them safe while you
collect the others.
